Our Mission

Why Femsex Boston?

Femsex Boston is a community-based organization focused on delivering The Femsex Workshop to the Boston community. Through this workshop, we seek to build a space for people to share their stories, to engage in honest and open dialogue, and to explore and challenge the experiences, the ideals, and the externalized and internalized structures that have been defining gender and sexual identity for generations.
